About Me

Hello! Thanks for checking out my website. Please forgive any glitches; I'm still working out how to HTML properly.

My name is Tristan Downing, and I grew up in Vancouver, Canada. I did the International Baccaleaureate Programme at Semiahmoo Secondary School, with a focus on the hard sciences. Based on my skills, this was a typical academic focus. I went on to represent Canada at the International Physics Olympics, and study mechanical engineering at the University of Cambridge, earning my BA in 2015 and my MEng in 2016. While I enjoyed the comfort of (relative) certainty in engineering, I always felt it was limited in scope, ultimately just a tool subservient to greater forces. While it may provide clear and useful solutions, the questions it answers are often not the important ones.

I always sought the more human applications of my technical skills. I worked with various NGOs, learning what I could.
